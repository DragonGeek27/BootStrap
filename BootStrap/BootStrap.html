Who am I?

Hi, my name is Joshua Kelly Werner. I was in the city of Boise, Idaho and was raised in the town of Meridian, Idaho. My K-12 years of school where tough. I did not fit in with any group and no one wanted me around. I was bullied consistently anywhere from being called names to being beat up, and even being told to go kill myself. When I graduated from high school I was uncertain about what I wanted to do, so I attended CWI where I received an associates in liberal arts. During my final year at CWI, I heard about the new GIMM program at Boise State. The good thing about attending a Junior College is that I could take a wide variety of classes to help me find an area of interest. I have always loved drawing and creating things, so I felt like it would be a good fit for me. I am currently working towards my 4-year degree at Boise State with a major in GIMM.
